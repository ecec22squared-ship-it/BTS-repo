An Immersive Text-Based RPG

Forget pre-rendered cutscenes. In Beyond the Stars, the only limit is your imagination. Every scene is written for you in real time — the hum of your freighter's engines, the flicker of a cantina's neon, the razor whisper of a plasma blade. You hear it. You feel it. You shape it.
