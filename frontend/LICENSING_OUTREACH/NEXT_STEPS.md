# 90-Day Action Plan (Realistic)

## Week 0 — This Week
- [ ] Read `README.md` and `CONTACTS.md` fully.
- [ ] Search for a games-industry IP attorney. Look for firms that mention “entertainment,” “games,” or “interactive media” on their site. LA- or NY-based is easiest.
- [ ] Book a **30–60 minute consultation** ($400–$800). Bring `LAWYER_BRIEF.md`.
- [ ] **Lock down the technical-demo preview URL** (Google-sign-in is required, confirm no one outside EC² can reach it).
- [ ] File EC² Gaming LLC if you haven't already. This gives the pitch gravitas.

## Week 1–2
- [ ] Incorporate attorney's redline into `PITCH_LETTER.md` and `PITCH_DECK.html`.
- [ ] Export the deck to PDF: open `PITCH_DECK.html` in Chrome, "Print" → "Save as PDF", file as `EC2_BeyondTheStars_Licensing_Inquiry.pdf`.
- [ ] Submit inquiry via https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/contact-us/ → "Legal Permissions and Image Licensing".
- [ ] Log the submission (date, form-submission ID, attached filename) in a timestamped note.

## Week 2–6
- [ ] **Hold.** Do not follow up, do not DM Disney executives on LinkedIn, do not mass-email the Lucasfilm press inbox. A single polite inquiry is professional. Two is spam.
- [ ] In parallel, start the **rebrand scoping**:
  - List every in-app string to change
  - Decide on the substitute lexicon (see `SUBMISSION_CHECKLIST.md` rename table)
  - Plan a rebrand build that keeps gameplay 100% intact

## Week 6–8
- [ ] If Disney replied with a lead: attorney leads negotiation. Do **not** call or email Disney directly.
- [ ] If Disney did not reply (most likely outcome): move to rebrand.

## Week 8–12
- [ ] Have the agent (main developer) run the rebrand pass.
- [ ] Update screenshots, store listing copy, and icon to match the rebranded universe.
- [ ] Submit the rebranded app to Apple App Store Connect + Google Play Console.
- [ ] Publish, start marketing via Discord + Instagram.

## After Launch (Month 4+)
- [ ] Collect download / retention / revenue metrics.
- [ ] Once at **50,000+ MAU and a 4.0+ average rating**, revisit the Disney licensing conversation — this time via a licensing agent, who will take your call if you have real traction.
- [ ] Consider a premium "Star Wars" skin as a *licensed* DLC, not a rebrand of the base app.

---

## Single most important thing on this page

> **Do not email anyone at Disney or Lucasfilm before your attorney has signed off on the exact wording you send, and do not publish the app with Star Wars marks until there is a signed license in hand.**

Everything else on this plan is reversible. Those two are not.
