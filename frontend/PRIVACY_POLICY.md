## 1. Purpose of the App

Beyond the Stars is a single-player, AI-narrated text role-playing game **for entertainment and amusement only**. The app is **not** intended for, and **must not be relied upon as**:

- Medical, psychological, or mental-health **treatment, diagnosis, or therapy**;
- Financial, investment, or legal **advice**;
- Market research, recruitment, or targeted **advertisement**;
- Educational certification of any kind.

All narrative content, dice outcomes, and in-game events are fictional and generated by AI models for recreational enjoyment.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information (via Google Sign-In)
When you sign in with Google, we receive:
- Your Google account **email address**
- Your **display name**
- Your **profile picture URL**
- A stable Google **user ID**

We do **not** receive your Google password and do not access your Gmail, contacts, or other Google services.

### 2.2 Gameplay Data
To run the game we store:
- Characters you create (name, species, career, backstory you write)
- Game-session state (current scene, journal, dice rolls)
- Coin balance, subscription tier, and unlocked content
- Aggregated, anonymized play-style metrics used only to tune future AI-generated scenarios for your own account

### 2.3 Payment Data
Subscriptions and coin purchases are processed by **Stripe**. We never see or store your full card number, CVC, or bank credentials. We retain only a transaction ID, amount, tier, and status to fulfill your order.

### 2.4 Technical Data
Standard server logs (IP, user-agent, timestamps) are retained for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and debugging.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data Used |
| --- | --- |
| Authenticate you on each launch | Google ID, session token |
| Save & restore your characters and adventures | Gameplay data |
| Generate AI narrative & illustrations | Character & session context (sent to the AI provider under contract) |
| Process purchases | Stripe transaction metadata |
| Fix bugs and prevent abuse | Technical logs |

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do **not** use your data for advertising or third-party marketing.

---

## 4. AI Providers

Narrative responses are generated by third-party large-language-model providers (including Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini, accessed via the Emergent Universal LLM service). Only the data needed to render the next scene (your current session context) is sent to these providers and is governed by their own privacy policies. No data is used by those providers to train public models on your behalf.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

- Account & gameplay data persist until you delete your account.
- To delete your account and associated data, email **universal4050@gmail.com** from the email address on file, or reach us in our Discord community. We will delete within 30 days.
- Backup copies are purged within 90 days of the primary deletion.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Beyond the Stars is rated **13+** for fantasy violence. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will remove it promptly.
