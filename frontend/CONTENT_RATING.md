## Google Play — IARC Questionnaire

Google uses a global questionnaire that produces ratings for multiple region boards (ESRB, PEGI, USK, etc.).

| Question group | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Does the app depict violence? | **Yes, fantasy/non-realistic violence** |
| Does the app depict realistic violence or injury? | **No** |
| Does the app contain sexual content? | **No** |
| Does the app contain nudity? | **No** |
| Does the app contain drug references? | **Yes, fictional substances (spice) in narrative** |
| Does the app contain gambling? | **No** |
| Does the app contain strong language? | **Mild, infrequent** |
| Does the app contain content that may disturb, frighten, or promote fear? | **No** |
| Does the app allow users to interact or exchange content? | **No** (single-player only; no chat, no UGC sharing outside of share-sheet) |
| Does the app share user's location with other users? | **No** |
| Does the app allow users to purchase digital items? | **Yes** |
| Does the app contain user-generated content? | **Yes** (own character backstories; not shared with other users in-app) |
| Does the app contain ads? | **No** |

**Expected IARC ratings:**
- ESRB (US): **T for Teen** (13+)
- PEGI (EU): **PEGI 12**
- USK (DE): **USK 12**
- ClassInd (BR): **12**
- ACB (AU): **PG**

## What to enter on both stores' "target age" field

**13 and older**

That aligns with the privacy policy and avoids COPPA (children's privacy) obligations.
