1. Purpose of the App

Beyond the Stars is a single-player, AI-narrated text role-playing game for entertainment and amusement only. The app is not intended for, and must not be relied upon as:

  • Medical, psychological, or mental-health treatment, diagnosis, or therapy
  • Financial, investment, or legal advice
  • Market research, recruitment, or targeted advertisement
  • Educational certification of any kind

All narrative content, dice outcomes, and in-game events are fictional and generated by AI models for recreational enjoyment.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information (via Google Sign-In)

When you sign in with Google, we receive: your Google account email address, display name, profile picture URL, and a stable Google user ID. We do not receive your Google password and do not access Gmail, contacts, or other Google services.

2.2 Gameplay Data

To run the game we store: characters you create (name, species, career, backstory you write), game-session state (current scene, journal, dice rolls), coin balance, subscription tier, unlocked content, and aggregated anonymized play-style metrics used only to tune future AI-generated scenarios for your own account.

2.3 Payment Data

Subscriptions and coin purchases are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number, CVC, or bank credentials. We retain only a transaction ID, amount, tier, and status.

2.4 Technical Data

Standard server logs (IP, user-agent, timestamps) are retained for up to 30 days for fraud prevention and debugging.

4. AI Providers

Narrative responses are generated by third-party large-language-model providers (Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, via the Emergent Universal LLM service). Only the data needed to render the next scene is sent and is governed by the providers' own privacy policies. No data is used by those providers to train public models on your behalf.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Account & gameplay data persist until you delete your account.
  • To delete your account, email universal4050@gmail.com from the email on file, or reach us on our Discord community. We will delete within 30 days.
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days of primary deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Beyond the Stars is rated 13+ for fantasy violence. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (EU/UK, California, etc.), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Email universal4050@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

HTTPS in transit, encrypted-at-rest MongoDB storage, and short-lived session tokens. If we become aware of a breach affecting your data we will notify you within 72 hours.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be posted inside the app and on our Discord. The "Last updated" date reflects the current version.
